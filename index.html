Empowering the Nation

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg.
Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have taken the amazing courses that Empowering
the Nation has to offer, from six-month learnerships to six-week short skills training courses.
These courses are designed to improve their skills and make them more marketable.
