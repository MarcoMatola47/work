Child Minding Course

Duration: 6 weeks
Fees: R750
Mode:In-person

About the course

This 6-week course is designed to provide basic child and baby care
knowledge, equipping participants with practical skills to care for
children from infancy to toddlerhood. This course is ideal for those
interested in childminding or enhancing their caregiving skills.

Purpose:

To provide a foundational understanding of child care for
different developmental stages, including baby and toddler care,
addressing essential needs, and promoting healthy development.

Course Content:

Birth to Six-Month-Old Baby Needs: Understanding the physical and emotional care requirements for infants.
Seven-Month to One-Year-Old Needs: Developmental milestones and caregiving strategies for older babies.
Toddler Needs: Providing appropriate care and support for toddlers, addressing both emotional and physical development.
Educational Toys: The role of toys in child development and how to select age-appropriate educational tools.
