Life Skills Course

Duration: 6 months
Fees: R1500
Mode: In-person

About the course

This 6-month course is aimed at equipping participants with essential life skills to navigate everyday situations and make informed decisions. It is ideal for individuals looking to gain practical knowledge for personal and professional development.

Purpose:

To provide participants with the necessary skills to handle basic life necessities, empowering them to manage personal finances, understand their rights, and develop essential literacy skills.

Course Content:

Opening a Bank Account: Learn the process and requirements for opening and managing a bank account.
Basic Labour Law: Understand your rights in the workplace and how to navigate employment contracts.
Basic Reading and Writing Literacy: Improve your ability to read and write for daily communication.
Basic Numeric Literacy: Develop skills to handle everyday calculations, budgeting, and financial decision-making.
