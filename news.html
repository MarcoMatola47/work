Our 1,000th Graduate from Empowering The Nation

In a remarkable milestone, Empowering the Nation proudly celebrated its 1,000th graduate this year. Since its inception in 2018, the organization has been dedicated to enhancing the skills and opportunities of domestic workers and gardeners in Johannesburg. This achievement underscores the positive impact of the six-month Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes offered by the organization.
The 1,000th graduate, Thandi Nkosi, shared her journey during the celebration event. "Before joining Empowering the Nation, I struggled to find stable work.
