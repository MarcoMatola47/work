Cooking Course

Duration: 6 weeks
Fees: R750
Mode:In-person

About the course

This 6-week course is designed to equip participants with essential
cooking skills, focusing on preparing nutritious meals for families. It
is ideal for individuals looking to improve their culinary abilities and
gain knowledge about balanced diets.

Purpose:

To teach participants how to prepare and cook nutritious meals, focusing
on the nutritional requirements for a better job, and better meal preparation.

Course Content:

Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body: Understanding the essential nutrients required for balanced diets.
Types of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables: A detailed look at various food groups and their importance.
Planning Meals: Techniques for organizing and planning nutritious meals.
Preparation and Cooking of Meals: Practical skills for preparing and cooking meals that meet nutritional needs.
