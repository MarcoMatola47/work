Garden maintenance Course

Duration: 6 weeks
Fees: R750
Mode:In-person

About the course

This 6-week course is designed to provide participants with the
essential skills and knowledge required to maintain a domestic garden.
Ideal for beginners, the course covers practical gardening techniques,
from watering to pruning and planting.

Purpose:

To equip participants with the knowledge needed to effectively water,
prune, and plant various types of plants in a domestic garden.

Course Content:

Water Restrictions and Requirements: Understanding the watering needs of both indigenous and exotic plants, while adhering to water restrictions.
Pruning and Propagation of Plants: Techniques for pruning and propagating plants to promote healthy growth.
Planting Techniques: Practical guidance on how to plant different types of plants, including proper soil preparation and planting methods.
