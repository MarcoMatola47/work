About us

Who are we

At Empowering the Nation, we believe in the transformative power of
education and skills development. Established in 2018, our organization
is dedicated to uplifting domestic workers and gardeners by providing
them with marketable skills that open doors to better job opportunities.
Based in Johannesburg, our six-month Learnerships and six-week Short
Skills Training Programmes equip participants with the knowledge and
confidence they need to thrive in today’s competitive job market.

What is our mission

Our mission is simple: to empower individuals with practical skills that
enhance their employability, foster independence, and improve their
quality of life. Through hands-on training and personalized support,
we’ve helped hundreds of people gain the tools to succeed, whether it’s
through learning new trades, expanding their skillsets, or pursuing
their dreams.

WHy we do it

With a commitment to community development, we continue to grow and
evolve, offering innovative solutions that meet the needs of our
learners and the industries they serve. At Empowering the Nation, we are
proud to be building a brighter, more empowered future for all.
