Landscaping Course

Duration: 6 months
Fees: R1500
Mode: In-person

About the course

This 6-month course is designed for those interested in creating and maintaining beautiful gardens. It is perfect for beginners and professionals looking to enhance their knowledge of plant care, garden design, and landscaping techniques.

Purpose:

To provide participants with the skills and knowledge needed to offer landscaping services for both new and established gardens, ensuring aesthetically pleasing and well-maintained outdoor spaces.

Course Content:

Indigenous and Exotic Plants and Trees: Learn to identify and care for a variety of plants and trees.
Fixed Structures: Understand the design and placement of garden elements such as fountains, statues, benches, and built-in braai.
Balancing Plants and Trees: Discover how to balance different types of plants and trees for a harmonious garden.
Aesthetics of Plant Shapes and Colours: Explore the visual aspects of garden design, focusing on plant shapes and color schemes.
Garden Layout: Learn how to design and plan the layout of a garden for optimal beauty and functionality.
