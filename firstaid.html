First aid Course

Duration: 6 months
Fees: R1500
Mode:In-person

About the course

This 6-month course is designed to provide participants with essential
first aid awareness and basic life support skills. It is ideal for
anyone looking to learn how to respond effectively to common emergencies
and provide life-saving assistance.

Purpose:

To equip participants with the knowledge and skills necessary to perform
first aid and basic life support, ensuring they can respond effectively
in emergency situations.

Course Content:

Wounds and Bleeding: How to manage and treat various types of wounds and control bleeding.
Burns and Fractures: Understanding how to assess and provide care for burns and fractures.
Emergency Scene Management: Steps to manage an emergency scene safely and efficiently.
Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR): Techniques for performing CPR in cases of cardiac arrest.
Respiratory Distress: Responding to respiratory emergencies such as choking or blocked airways.
